Our Solution

Introducing MooMe —
An Affordable AI-IoT Platform

MooMe is an AI-powered, IoT-integrated dairy farm management mobile application — purpose-built for smallholder farms. It runs on standard Android smartphones, integrates with low-cost IoT kits ( to reduce between 40% to 60% vs. $2,000/farm for alternatives), and delivers actionable intelligence through an intuitive mobile interface.

System Architecture

MooMe — IoT-AI Livestock
Monitoring & Automated Control

10 integrated subsystems: smart cow sensors (RFID, temperature, heart rate, activity), environment sensors, central Raspberry Pi controller, automated feeding & water, milking system, cloud database, AI analytics, dashboard & mobile app, and SMS alerts — all solar-powered.
