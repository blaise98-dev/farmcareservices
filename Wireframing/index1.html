MooMe is a mobile monitoring & measurement IoT-AI platform transforming Rwanda's dairy sector. Piloted across 6 districts in two milk sheds — Nyagatare (extensive) & Burera (intensive) — it equips 1,200 dairy farmers with smart collars, sensors, and an AI-powered app in Kinyarwanda & English for real-time disease detection, heat monitoring, feed optimization & data-driven decisions.

Challenges
! Problem
  • Low milk yields: 5–10L/day vs 25–35L potential
  • Late disease detection — high vet costs & mortality
  • Feed costs = 60–70% of production expenses
  • Missed heat detection & long calving intervals
  • Livestock theft in extensive grazing systems
  • No farm data — decisions based on guesswork
  • Weak value chain (farmers ↔ processors)
  • Limited extension access — no digital tools
  • Climate stress with no adaptive systems
